# Dataset Notice

Sightline includes a small local sample fixture for testing the dashboard and calibration flow without a real camera.

## Included Sample Data

Tracked sample files:

- `sample-data/pklot/preview.jpg`
- `sample-data/pklot/overlay.jpg`
- `sample-data/pklot/slots.json`
- `sample-data/pklot/manifest.json`

These files are intended for local demonstration and review.

## Local-Only Files

The repo may contain ignored local media files on a developer machine, such as MP4 test clips or larger reference images. Those files are not part of the committed repository unless explicitly added.

## User Responsibilities

Anyone adding camera footage, images, annotations, datasets, or model weights is responsible for ensuring they have the right to use them.

Before adding or deploying data, verify:

- Dataset license terms.
- Camera ownership and access rights.
- Privacy requirements.
- Local laws and policies.
- Consent or signage requirements where applicable.
- Rules for storing, sharing, or processing footage.

## Privacy Notes

Parking lot footage can include vehicles, license plates, people, business locations, and timestamps. Treat it as sensitive operational data.

Avoid committing private camera footage, credentials, internal RTSP URLs, or personally identifying information.
